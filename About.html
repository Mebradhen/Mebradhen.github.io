    About


    Wecome to the End... The EnderLost



    EnderLost Studios is a Brisbane Base indie game company. Since being conceptualised in 2013 a range of games have been created. The most successful being An Aspie Life. We are always trying to improve our products with every passing year. How do we make our products? In one little room inside a little house. The speakers next to a PC blast the greatest hit of the 70’s, 80’s and 90’s. The room is coloured blue from the LED on the wall. The Nixie clock ticks… and a world of ideas are realized. Whether that is with code, animation or models. The final product will be something entertaining and fun to play or watch. Every new idea has dedication, love and craft put into every second of the product.



    With 5 years of game development and animation experience, we know how to make a fun product. Our goal is to entertain.


    We Are Located in Brisbane Australia… There are no deadly animals
