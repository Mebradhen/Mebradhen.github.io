It's the Final Exchange

In any lasting relationship, it’s unusual for the breakup to be the last meeting. More often than not, it’s the final exchange of belongings. Sometimes, it’s simple. ‘Can I have my jacket back?’ But sometimes, it’s more complicated…

Final Exchange is a 2.5D point and click adventure game aimed at repairing a relationship!

This was originally made for the Global Game Jam 2020 in less than 48 hours!
